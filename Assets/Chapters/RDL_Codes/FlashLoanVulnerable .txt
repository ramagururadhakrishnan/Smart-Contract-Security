FlashLoanVulnerable 

RDL for this code in SmartCheck can include rules such as:

Requiring a function for checking the borrower's creditworthiness before lending funds.
Implementing a timeout for repayments to prevent indefinite usage.
Adding a maximum loan amount to limit the potential impact of flash loans.
Implementing a mechanism for penalizing late or missing repayments.
Ensuring that the lender's funds are stored in a secure, decentralized contract.
